Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Japan?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Many countries have visa-waiver agreements with Japan for stays up to 90 days for tourism purposes. Check with your local Japanese embassy or consulate for specific requirements.

In countryside in Japan expensive to visit?

Not really. It'd sometimes cost to get there, but you do not need to pay anything once you get there and get along with locals in many cases. They like feeding you and even letting you sleep over in their house.

Do I need to speak Japanese?

Don't worry. You don't have to learn normalize Japanese. Learning each dialect that locals speak is a way more helpful to you.

Is Japan safe for travelers?

As long as you'd not go out in the evening, it should be safe. You should be careful about wildanimals which take a nightwalk around the place.
